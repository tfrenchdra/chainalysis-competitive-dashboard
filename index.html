AIO Strategic Takeaways

⚠ Threat: TRM Labs Glossary Dominance

TRM Labs generates 7.6x more AIO traffic than Chainalysis (8,924 vs 1,178/mo) largely through glossary pages like "fiat currency" (1,569 traffic), "fiat money" (112), and "blockchain analytics" (169). Their definitional content is being surfaced by Google's AI as the authoritative answer.

💡 Opportunity: Glossary & Educational Content

Chainalysis has zero glossary pages capturing AI Overviews. Building a comprehensive glossary targeting terms like "blockchain forensics," "fiat currency," and "virtual asset service provider" could unlock thousands of monthly AIO visits.

✅ Strength: Brand & Product Terms

Chainalysis dominates AIOs for branded and product-adjacent terms like "chain analysis" (356 traffic), "crypto compliance" (125), and "crypto investigations" (50). These high-intent queries show strong brand authority in AI results.

📈 Growth Play: High-Volume AIO Gaps

TRM Labs captures AIOs on queries totaling 61,210 monthly searches vs Chainalysis at 5,880. The gap is almost entirely from educational/definitional content. Targeting just the top 5 TRM glossary terms could add 2,000+ monthly AIO visits.

Critical TRM Labs Glossary Strategy Is Capturing High-Volume Keywords

TRM Labs has built a systematic glossary section that now ranks #1 for high-value terms like "fiat currency" (26K vol), "fiat money" (18K vol), "stablecoin" (17K vol), "decentralized finance" (16K vol), and "what is fiat money" (5.6K vol). Their top 5 glossary pages alone drive 16,100+ monthly visits — nearly 40% of their total organic traffic. Chainalysis has no equivalent glossary or definition content targeting these terms.

5 glossary pages in top 3 ▲ 16,100+ monthly visits from glossary fiat currency: TRM #1, Chain. not ranking

Recommendation: Launch a comprehensive glossary/learn section targeting foundational crypto and blockchain terms. Prioritize "fiat currency," "stablecoin," "decentralized finance," and "blockchain technology" — all 15K+ volume terms where TRM currently owns the SERP.

Critical Chainalysis Traffic Declining — TRM Labs Closing the Gap

Chainalysis US organic traffic has declined 39% over 14 months (40,721 → 24,754) while TRM Labs has grown 32% (14,832 → 19,550). The traffic gap has narrowed from 25,889 visits to just 5,204. Chainalysis still leads but the trajectory is concerning. Both domains have seen Top 3 keyword erosion in US results, with Chainalysis at 567 and TRM at 524.

Traffic gap: 5,204 ▲ was 25,889 Chain. Top 3: 567 vs TRM: 524 TRM growth rate: +32% over 14 months

Recommendation: Audit declining Chainalysis pages to identify content decay. Refresh high-traffic posts losing rankings and expand content velocity to match TRM's output cadence. Prioritize defending top-traffic pages like the stablecoin and crypto regulation posts.

Critical TRM Labs Backlink Acquisition Rate Is 3x Faster

TRM Labs referring domains grew from 3,319 to 9,536 — a 187% increase over 14 months (+6,217 new referring domains). Chainalysis grew from 18,709 to 23,735 (+27%, +5,026 domains). While Chainalysis still leads in absolute count (23,735 vs 9,536), TRM's acquisition rate is higher in absolute terms and 7x higher by percentage. Their DR has climbed from 70 to 75 over 14 months, closing the authority gap to just 8 points.

TRM: +6,217 ref domains (187%) ▲ Chain: +5,026 ref domains (27%) DR gap: 8 points (was 11)

Recommendation: Invest in digital PR and link-building campaigns to maintain the authority moat. Focus on data-driven research reports and industry studies that naturally attract editorial links. Monitor TRM's backlink sources to identify link opportunities they're capitalizing on.

Warning Chainalysis Top 3 Keywords Dropping — Quality of Rankings Declining

Chainalysis US Top 3 keywords currently stand at 567, while TRM Labs holds 524. Both domains have seen erosion in premium US rankings. With Chainalysis at just 567 Top 3 positions, the quality of rankings remains a concern — indicating that content freshness and optimization are needed to maintain and recover high-value positions.

Chain. Top 3: 567 (US) TRM Top 3: 524 (US)

Recommendation: Conduct a position-loss audit — identify specific keywords that dropped from positions 1-3 to 4-10+. Refresh content with updated data, improved structure, and stronger internal linking. Consider whether competitors are providing more comprehensive or up-to-date answers on these queries.

Warning Elliptic Viral Content Strategy Drives Massive Search Volume Spikes

Elliptic achieved a massive traffic spike in late 2025 (peaking at 47,499 US visits in Jan 2026, up from 4,418 in Feb 2025) driven by viral blog content, particularly the AI deepfake/undresser article (3,688 monthly US visits) and regulatory content like the Clarity Act coverage. While the spike was temporary (now back to 7,632), Elliptic's referring domains grew permanently from 6,731 to 11,569 (+72%), and they now hold a larger referring domain base than TRM Labs.

Elliptic Jan 2026 peak: 47,499 US visits Ref domains: +72% permanent growth Top page: 3,688 visits (AI deepfake)

Recommendation: Develop a newsjacking and rapid-response content strategy for trending crypto/regulatory topics. Elliptic's approach of publishing timely, newsworthy analysis on emerging issues generates both traffic spikes and permanent backlink gains. Chainalysis's brand authority positions it well to become the go-to source for breaking crypto compliance analysis.

Warning SOV Share Declining — Chainalysis Now Under 45%

Chainalysis's share of US organic traffic among these three competitors has declined from 68.0% (Feb 2025) to 48.0% (Apr 2026). TRM Labs grew from 24.8% to 37.9%. While Chainalysis still leads, the trajectory shows a clear convergence pattern driven by Chainalysis's traffic decline and TRM's steady growth.

Chain. SOV: 68.0% → 48.0% ▼ TRM SOV: 24.8% → 37.9% ▲ Elliptic SOV: 7.4% → 14.8% (post-spike normalization)

Recommendation: This is a compound effect of all the threats above. Addressing the glossary gap, content freshness, and backlink growth rate should stabilize SOV decline. Consider setting a quarterly SOV target and monitoring against it.

Opportunity Chainalysis Authority Moat Remains Strong — Leverage It

Despite the competitive threats, Chainalysis still holds significant structural advantages: DR 83 (vs 75 for both competitors), 23,735 referring domains (2.5x TRM, 2x Elliptic), 4.2M live backlinks (40x TRM), and the highest US traffic value at $32.9K (2.9x TRM). The brand query "chainalysis" continues to drive strong branded search volume. This authority foundation means new content from Chainalysis should rank faster and higher than competitor content — if produced.

DR advantage: +8 over both competitors Backlink advantage: 40x over TRM Traffic value: 2.9x TRM (US) Ref domain advantage: 2.5x TRM

Recommendation: The authority is there — Chainalysis needs to produce more content to leverage it. A glossary launch, increased blog cadence, and topical hub pages would compound on existing authority. Prioritize content in areas where TRM's glossary is winning with relatively shallow pages that Chainalysis can outperform with deeper, more authoritative content.

Opportunity AEO / AI Citation Positioning

As AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) increasingly source from authoritative domains, Chainalysis's DR 83 and deep backlink profile position it strongly for AI citation capture. However, this requires content that directly answers common questions in a structured, authoritative format — exactly the type of glossary and FAQ content that TRM Labs is already producing. Without this content layer, Chainalysis risks being overlooked by AI systems in favor of competitors with more comprehensive definitional content.

AI citation potential: High (DR 83) Current AEO-ready content: Low Competitor AEO content: TRM glossary, Elliptic blockchain-basics

Recommendation: Build structured, question-and-answer format content targeting high-volume definitional queries. Add FAQ schema markup to existing blog posts. Create a "blockchain basics" or "crypto compliance guide" section with clear, concise definitions that AI systems can easily extract and cite. Monitor AI citation presence with Ahrefs Brand Radar once configured.
